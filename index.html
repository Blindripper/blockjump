Game Mechanics

BlockJump is a vertical platformer where you climb as high as you can while collecting powerups and avoiding hazards.

  • Use arrow keys to move left and right, and jump.
  • Collect powerups to gain special abilities or score boosts.
  • Avoid red spike platforms - they'll end your game!
  • The higher you climb, the higher your score.
  • Wind effects will push you left or right - adapt your jumps!
  • Try to achieve special milestones to unlock achievements.
